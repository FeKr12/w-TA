BACKGROUND

Ture Andersson is an artist who cuts and pastes with his razor sharp teeth and sticky tongue. He currently resides on Gotland with his owner.

Before he became an artist he was a regular guy, back then he had no worries in life and spent his days eating bugs and basking under the heat lamp.
